Rice Pilaf with Chorizo and Scallions
1  6- to 8-ounce box rice pilaf
4 ounces Spanish chorizo (cured sausage), sliced
4  scallions, chopped
Cook the rice pilaf according to the package directions. Meanwhile, in a medium skillet, over medium-high heat, cook the chorizo until just browned, 1 to 2 minutes.Stir into the pilaf along with the scallions before serving.
