Chicken and Corn Two Ways Tostada
1  6-inch whole-grain corn tortilla
2 ounces shredded skinless rotisserie chicken breast
¼ cup pickled onions
¼ cup sweet corn kernels
1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
Bake tortilla on oven rack at 350° for 8 to 10 minutes or until crisp. Top with chicken, pickled onions, corn, and lime juice.
