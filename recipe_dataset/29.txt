Cajun Butter
6 tablespoons unsalted butter
3 tablespoons finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons finely chopped red bell pepper
2 tablespoons finely chopped celery
2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
¼ cup fresh cilantro leaves
Melt 6 tablespoons butter in a small skillet over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, celery, and Cajun seasoning; cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Remove skillet from heat; add 2 tablespoons butter. Transfer mixture to a mini food processor, and process until smooth. Let cool to room temp­erature. (Sprinkle corn with cilantro leaves before serving.)
