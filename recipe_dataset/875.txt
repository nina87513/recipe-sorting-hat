Cheesy Pita Chips
4  (6-inch) whole-wheat pita rounds
4 teaspoons olive oil
¼ cup grated Manchego cheese
½ teaspoon dried oregano
.38 teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon garlic powder
.13 teaspoon ground red pepper
Preheat oven to 400°. Split pita rounds in half horizontally; brush rough side of each with 1/2 teaspoon olive oil. Cut each into 8 wedges. Arrange in single layers on 2 baking sheets. Combine Manchego cheese, oregano, salt, garlic powder, and ground red pepper in a bowl. Sprinkle pita with mixture. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown, rotating pans halfway through.
