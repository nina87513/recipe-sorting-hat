Chicken Chow Mein
¼ cup butter or margarine
4 cups chopped cooked chicken
1  medium onion, chopped
3  celery ribs, chopped
1  medium-size green bell pepper, chopped
2  (10 3/4-ounce) cans cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
¼ cup soy sauce
1  (2-ounce) jar diced pimiento, undrained
1  (5-ounce) can chow mein noodles
1 cup salted cashews
Cook butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring often, until lightly browned. Add chicken and next 6 ingredients; cook, stirring often, 10 to 12 minutes. Serve over noodles, and sprinkle with salted cashews.
