Parmesan Won Ton Crisps
¼ cup butter or margarine, melted
20  won ton skins
½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
Brush 2 baking sheets with butter. Cut won tons in half diagonally; place in a single layer on baking sheets. Brush won tons with remaining butter; sprinkle with cheese.
Bake at 375° for 5 to 6 minutes or until golden.
