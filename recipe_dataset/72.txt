Chicken Lettuce Wraps
2  garlic cloves
1 pound skinned and boned chicken breast halves, coarsely chopped
2 tablespoons dark sesame oil
Â¼ cup chopped fresh mint
2  green onions, chopped
2 tablespoons soy sauce
8  large iceberg lettuce leaves
Garnish: fresh mint leaves
Pulse garlic in food processor 3 times or until minced. Add chicken, and pulse 8 times or until ground.
Cook chicken mixture in hot oil in a large skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes or until chicken is done. Stir in mint, onions, and soy sauce. Serve on lettuce; garnish, if desired.
