Sauteed Mushrooms with Garlic
1 tablespoon olive oil
1  (4-ounce) package gourmet mushroom blend
2  garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1 cup Israeli-Style Hummus
¼ teaspoon kosher salt
¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms; sauté 4 minutes. Add garlic; cook 2 minutes. Top Israeli-Style Hummus with mushroom mixture; sprinkle with salt and pepper.
