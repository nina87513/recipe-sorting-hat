Smoky Egg and Cheese Tostada
1  6-inch whole-grain corn tortilla
1  large fried egg
2 tablespoons chipotle salsa
1 tablespoon shredded cheddar cheese
1½ teaspoons thinly sliced green onions
Bake tortilla on oven rack at 350° for 8 to 10 minutes or until crisp. Top with fried egg, chipotle salsa, cheese, and green onions.
