Sparkling White Sangria
1  Granny Smith apple, cored, thinly sliced, slices quartered
1  red apple, cored, thinly sliced, slices quartered
1  orange, washed, thinly sliced, slices quartered, seeds removed
1  lemon, washed, thinly sliced, slices quartered, seeds removed
1  750ml bottle dry white wine, chilled
1 cup triple sec or other orange liqueur
1 cup fresh orange juice (from 4 large oranges)
1  liter club soda, chilled
In a large pitcher combine fruits, wine, triple sec and orange juice. Cover and chill for at least 8 hours. To serve, fill 8 glasses with ice, divide fruit and wine mixture evenly among glasses and top off with club soda.
