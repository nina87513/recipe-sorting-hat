Tortilla Soup
2  14- to 19-ounce cans chicken noodle soup
1 cup frozen corn kernels
1 teaspoon hot sauce
2 cups tortilla chips
1  avocado, cut into pieces
In a medium saucepan, bring the soup to a simmer. Add the corn and hot sauce and cook, stirring occasionally, just until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Ladle into bowls and serve with tortilla chips and avocado.
